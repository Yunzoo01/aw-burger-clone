We’re committed to sourcing simple, great-tasting ingredients that are crafted with care. That’s why we’ve completely eliminated processed cheese from our menu. At A&W we only serve real cheese made without artificial flavours, colours or preservatives..

We serve three different types of cheese: real cheddar cheese, real mozzarella cheese and real cheese curds. Most of our burgers and breakfast sandwiches are served with real cheddar cheese. But on other menu items, we also serve mozzarella cheese (served on our Mozza Burger®) and cheese curds (served on our Poutine).

We are proud to support Canadian dairy farmers and work with amazing cheesemakers to serve real, natural cheeses in all our restaurants.

Real cheese is made with simple ingredients like milk, starter culture, salt and microbial enzymes. It’s made without any artificial flavours, colours or preservatives. Our real cheddar cheese is much lower in sodium than typical processed cheese and it’s naturally aged to develop the perfect medium cheddar flavour our guests know and love.

Real cheese is made with milk and milk ingredients. So, it's important that people with dairy sensitivities take caution when eating. Browse through our menu page for more information on ingredients and allergens.

As of December 31, 2018, we completely eliminated plastic straws from all of our restaurants. We now only carry paper straws. We’re the first fast-food restaurant in all of North America to do this and we’re pretty excited about it. We truly believe that small changes like this can have a big impact on our planet.

Replacing straws was one big, exciting step in our waste reduction plan, but we know there's more to do. Finding practical ways to reduce waste is one of our top priorities. Out of all the packaging in our restaurants, plastic straws were the most problematic. Unlike other recyclable plastics, like takeout cup lids, straws are not accepted in most waste processing facilities.

We worked closely with our supplier to ensure that A&W's paper straws would provide the same great sipping experience as plastic straws. Believe it or not, paper straws have been around since the 1880s, but with recent advancements in cutting-edge straw technology, their quality has soared to new heights. Our paper straws will last for about 2–3 hours in a drink and will biodegrade in about 3–6 months.

A&W’s paper straws are 100% biodegradable, compostable and sustainably sourced. They’re accepted wherever compostable facilities exist and can even be placed in organic waste bins.

By introducing paper straws, we’re able to eliminate 82 million plastic straws from landfills every year. All of our straws are 100% biodegradable.
